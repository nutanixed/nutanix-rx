Automated startup

                

            

            

                
This runs all ten steps from Help → Startup Sequence with built-in wait periods. Total time is often well over an hour depending on cluster behavior.

                
Output appears in Live Logs. You can leave this page open until the run finishes.

            

            

                
                
            

        

    

    
        

            

                

                    
                

                

                    
Shut down entire environment?

                    
Automated shutdown

                

            

            

                
This runs the full shutdown sequence from Help (including the NDK webhook safeguard, then NKP through host power-off). Workloads will be stopped and hosts powered off via Redfish.

                
Ensure no one relies on this cluster before continuing. Recovery requires physical or out-of-band access if something fails mid-sequence.
